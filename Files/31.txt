My current research encompasses the design of approximation algorithms for (i) computing diffuse reflection paths in polygons,&nbsp; and (ii) computing vertex guard sets for guarding polygons. I am also working on estimating bounds on the sizes of separating and bisecting families for set systems (hypergraphs).
