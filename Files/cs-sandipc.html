My research interests span the domains of wireless networks, mobile/smart-phone computing and distributed computing. I am particularly interested in system development for communication ranging from broadband wireless to smart city communication. My present research focuses are broadly in following areas:
1. High Throughput Wireless Backbone Networks
2. Navigation System for Developing Countries based on Smartphone/Wearable Sensors
3. Traffic Analytics over Smart-phone App Data
4. Mobile Access Networks - Traffic Shaping and Optimization
5. Software Defined Networking and Network Function Virtualization

I am also a member of Systems and Mobile research group at the Department of CSE, and also associated with the Complex Network Research Group (CNeRG).
