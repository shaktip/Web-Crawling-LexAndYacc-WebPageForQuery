My current research interests are Computer Security and Data Analytics. Over the years, I have also worked in the fields of Image Processing and Pattern Recognition.

Frontal Gait Recognition from Occluded Scenes by P. Chattopadhyay, Shamik Sural, J. Mukherjee Pattern Recognition Letters Vol. 63(1) pp. 9-15 (2015)

Meeting Cardinality Constraints in Role Mining by P. Harika, M. Nagajyothi, John C. John, Shamik Sural, Jaideep Vaidya and Vijay Atluri IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing 12(1) pp. 71 - 84 (2015)

The Generalized Temporal Role Mining Problem by B. Mitra, Shamik Sural, V. Atluri, J. Vaidya Journal of Computer Security Vol. 23(1) pp. 31-56 (2015)

Modeling, Synthesis and Characterization of Occlusion in Videos by Aditi Roy, Pratik Chattopadhyay, Shamik Sural, J. Mukherjee and Gerhard Rigoll IET Computer Vision 9(6) pp. 821-830 (2015)

Information Fusion from Multiple Cameras for Gait-based Re-identification and Recognition by P. Chattopadhyay, Shamik Sural, J. Mukherjee IET Image Processing Vol. 9(11) pp. 969-976 (2015)

Minimizing Organizational User Requirement while Meeting Security Constraints by Arindam Roy, Shamik Sural, Arun Kumar Majumdar, Jaideep Vaidya and Vijay Atluri ACM Transactions on Management Information Systems 6(3), Article No. 12 (2015)
