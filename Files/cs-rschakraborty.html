My research interests are in the areas of Hardware Security, Application of Novel Nano-devices to Hardware Security, VLSI Design and Methodologies and Digital Multimedia Security. I am part of the "Secured Embedded Architecture Laboratory" (SEAL), where we focus on applied security research. For more details about my publications, please refer to https://sites.google.com/site/rschakraborty/ and https://sites.google.com/site/rschakraborty/.
